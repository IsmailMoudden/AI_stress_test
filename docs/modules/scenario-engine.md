# Scenario Engine

## Purpose

The Scenario Engine will convert approved assumptions into structured, explainable market shock proposals.

## Future Responsibilities

- map scenario assumptions to market factors
- propose shocks and propagation logic
- manage correlation assumptions
- expose confidence and rationale
- preserve human overrides

## Non-Responsibilities

- pricing model implementation
- portfolio revaluation
- market data licensing decisions
- final approval of scenarios

## Governance Requirements

Generated shocks must be explainable, versioned, and editable before they can become approved scenario inputs.
