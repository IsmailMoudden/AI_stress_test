# Stress Engine Integration

## Purpose

The Stress Engine module will integrate Scenario Workbench with existing approved pricing and risk engines.

## Future Responsibilities

- submit approved scenario inputs
- track execution requests
- receive official risk outputs
- reconcile execution status
- retain references to risk engine runs

## Non-Responsibilities

- implementing valuation models
- replacing approved risk engines
- inventing official PnL or exposure numbers

## Governance Requirements

Only approved scenario inputs should be sent to valuation systems. All execution requests and results must be traceable.
